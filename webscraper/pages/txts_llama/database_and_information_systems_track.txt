Database and Information Systems Track
Objectives
Prepares students to apply database principles, algorithms, and optimization techniques to design, build, and manage current
and future database and information systems.
All major required courses, all track requirements and track selectives, and their pre-requisites, regardless
of department, must be completed with a grade of C or better.
