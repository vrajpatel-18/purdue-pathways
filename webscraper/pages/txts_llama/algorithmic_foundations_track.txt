Algorithmic Foundations Track
Objectives
The Algorithmic Foundations track gives students a broad education on foundational concepts, tools, and techniques
underlying existing and future areas of computer science.
All major required courses, all track requirements and track selectives, and their pre-requisites, regardless
of department, must be completed with a grade of C or better.
